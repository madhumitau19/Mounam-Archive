Love Letter: Dane to Amara
Dane Redwood

"Dear Amara, this silence, not being able to tell you how I feel, how I love you, how I miss you, how I long to hold you, it is almost impossible to continue, but I do so for you. If not to see you and hear your voice, then to imagine you, the things we would say to each other. We must believe that even now we can build something, something beyond fear, beyond this oppression. That we still can feel this love, know this truth without even saying a word of it, it is reason enough to hope. I love you. I love you. Dane"
