The Music of Mounam


The Mounam Empire’s reign was a dark time for all- a time of extreme censorship, surveillance and cultural loss; a time of silence.

But in the wake of such cruelty and unprecedented abuse of power, we as a people persevered- through the only means still accessible: music.

In the absence of speech, true connection and communication, musicians filled the gaps with their craft, and in the process of producing this coded form of communication, created one of the most distinctive and enigmatic eras of music.This is an archive intended to preserve the music and documents of the period and honor the musicians who were instrumental in bringing us hope when we needed it the most, and eventually returning us to freedom and normalcy.
